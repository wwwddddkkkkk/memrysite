Frequently asked

What is Memry?
Memry is a personal collection app that helps you organize things you love, collect, experience, or want to remember. It's a home for the small worlds you keep building — cafés, cities, books, souvenirs, anything.
Is Memry a photo storage app?
No. Memry is designed around personal collections. Photos can be part of an item, but the focus is organizing memories, places, objects, and experiences beautifully.
How do I request account or data deletion?
Memry uses Sign in with Apple and stores your collections in your own iCloud. You can delete any collection or item directly inside the app, and revoke Sign in with Apple access from your Apple ID settings at any time. To also delete the limited identifier we hold for your account, email support@memryworld.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request."
Where are my collections stored?
In your own iCloud, through Apple's CloudKit framework. That means they sync between your Apple devices automatically, and Memry doesn't keep a separate copy on its own servers. See the Privacy Policy for details.
How do I report a bug?
Email support@memryworld.com with your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue. Screenshots help.
How do I send feedback or feature ideas?
Email support@memryworld.com. We love hearing how people collect and remember things.
When will Memry be available?
Memry is preparing for App Store review. For the most up-to-date launch information, contact info@memryworld.com.
Which devices does Memry support?
Memry is designed for iPhone (iPhone 12 and later, iOS 17+). Other platforms aren't planned for the initial launch.

Data & deletion

Memry uses Sign in with Apple and iCloud, so most of your data lives in your own iCloud account, not on Memry's servers. You can:

  • Delete individual items or whole collections directly inside the app — they're removed from your iCloud immediately.
  • Revoke Sign in with Apple access at any time in Settings → Apple ID → Sign-In & Security → Sign in with Apple.
  • Email support@memryworld.com with the subject Data Deletion Request to also delete the limited account identifier we hold.

For full details, see the Privacy Policy.

Bug reports

To help us reproduce the issue, please include:

  • Device model (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro)
  • iOS version (e.g. iOS 17.4)
  • Memry app version (Settings → About)
  • A short description of what you were doing and what happened
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if you have one

Send everything to support@memryworld.com. We aim to respond within two business days.
