Memry ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Memry handles information when you use our mobile application and website.

Where your data lives

Memry is local-first. Your collections live on your device, and optionally sync through your own iCloud — never on our servers.

You sign in with Sign in with Apple. Your collections, items, photos, notes, dates, and locations are stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud sync turned on for Memry, they also sync across your devices through your own iCloud (CloudKit). Either way, Memry does not run a separate cloud copy of your content. You stay in control of your iCloud through Apple's privacy settings at all times.

1. Information we collect

Memry is designed to collect as little as possible. Depending on how you use the app, the following information may be involved:

From Sign in with Apple

When you sign in, Apple shares a unique identifier and the name and email address you choose to share (which may be a private relay address generated by Apple). We use this only to identify your account inside the app. You can revoke this access at any time in your Apple ID settings.

If you choose Hide My Email during sign-in, Memry will only ever see and contact you at the @privaterelay.appleid.com address Apple generates — we never receive your real email address.

Stored on your device — optionally synced through your iCloud

Your user-created content — collection names, item names, notes, photos, dates, locations, tags, and visual preferences — is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud sync turned on for Memry, it also syncs across your devices through Apple's CloudKit framework, in your own iCloud account. Either way, Memry does not maintain a separate copy on its own infrastructure. Access to any synced data is governed by Apple's terms and your iCloud settings.

Device & app information

We may collect basic diagnostic information — app version, device type, operating system version, and anonymous crash reports — to improve app performance and stability. Where supported by iOS, this is shared via Apple's standard crash reporting tools and is not personally identifying.

Support information

If you contact us, we receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this only to respond to your request.

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Authenticate your Memry account through Sign in with Apple.
  • Provide and operate Memry's core features.
  • Allow your collections and items to sync across your devices through your own iCloud, if you have iCloud sync turned on for Memry.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Improve app stability, design, and functionality.
  • Protect the security and integrity of the service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. User content

Your collections, photos, notes, locations, and other personal content belong to you. They live on your device and, if you have iCloud sync turned on for Memry, in your own iCloud account. We do not access, sell, share, or monetize them. We do not train any model on your content.

4. Photos, camera, and location

Photos and camera. With your permission, Memry can access your photo library to attach an existing photo to an item, or use your camera to take a new photo to attach. Photos you add — including any embedded metadata they may carry (such as date, time, or GPS coordinates) — are stored locally on your device, and synced to your other devices through your own iCloud if iCloud sync is on. Memry does not upload your photos to its own servers, does not share them, and does not analyze them for any purpose other than displaying them in your collection.

Location. With your permission, Memry can tag an item with your current location. Location data is stored locally on your device, and synced through your own iCloud if iCloud sync is on.

Revocation. You can revoke camera, photo library, or location permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Memry, without affecting items you have already saved.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Because your content lives on your device and (optionally) in your own iCloud, there is little for us to share. We may share limited information only when necessary to:

  • Operate the app through Apple's platform services (Sign in with Apple, iCloud / CloudKit, push notifications).
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Comply with applicable laws or valid legal processes.
  • Protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the service.

6. Third-party services

Memry is built on Apple platform services and keeps third parties to a minimum:

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, iCloud, CloudKit, push notifications, App Store, TestFlight, crash reporting. Apple's handling of this data is governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.
  • Optional services — if we later add anything beyond Apple's platform (for example, an email provider for support replies), we will list it here and update this policy.

We do not use third-party advertising, ad networks, or cross-site trackers.

7. Data retention

Because your content is stored on your device and (optionally) in your own iCloud, you control how long it is kept. If you delete a collection or item inside the app, it is removed from your device immediately, and from your iCloud copy if iCloud sync is on. If you uninstall the app without iCloud sync, the local copy is removed with the app.

The limited identifiers we receive through Sign in with Apple are kept only as long as needed to operate your account. We keep support correspondence as long as reasonably necessary to handle your request and comply with legal obligations.

8. Data deletion requests

You can delete your data in two ways:

  • Inside the app. Delete individual items or full collections — they are removed from your device immediately, and from your iCloud copy if iCloud sync is on. You can also delete your entire Memry account from inside the app: this removes the identifier we hold for you and revokes Memry's Sign in with Apple authorization on your behalf via Apple's token-revocation service. Separately, you can revoke Sign in with Apple access at any time in your Apple ID settings.
  • By email. Contact support@memryworld.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request." We will delete any identifier we hold and confirm by email. We may need to verify your request first.

We will respond to verified deletion requests within 30 days.

9. Your rights and international data

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding personal information we hold about you, including the right to access, correct, port, or delete it, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact support@memryworld.com.

Because your collections, items, photos, notes, dates, and locations live on your device and (optionally) in your own iCloud, you can exercise most of these rights directly on the device or through Apple's iCloud and Apple ID settings. Memry itself only holds your Sign in with Apple identifier, the email address Apple shares with us, any support correspondence, and anonymous diagnostics.

California residents (CCPA / CPRA). We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. You have the right to know what we collect, to delete it, to correct it, and to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

EEA, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR / UK GDPR). We rely on your consent (for example, Sign in with Apple, photos, and location permissions) and on the performance of our agreement with you (to operate the app) as our legal bases for processing. Any limited personal data we hold is processed under Apple's standard contractual arrangements for any transfers outside your region.

10. Children's privacy

Memry is not directed to children under 13, or the equivalent minimum age of digital consent in the user's jurisdiction (for example, 16 in parts of the EU and UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below those ages. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact support@memryworld.com.

11. Security

Memry relies on Apple's platform security: Sign in with Apple, encrypted iCloud storage and transport, and the iOS sandbox. We add reasonable safeguards on our side for the limited information we receive. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

13. Contact us

For privacy questions, support requests, or data deletion requests, contact support@memryworld.com.

For general inquiries, contact info@memryworld.com.
