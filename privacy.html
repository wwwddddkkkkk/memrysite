Memry ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Memry handles information when you use our mobile application and website.

Where your data lives

Memry uses Apple's privacy tools. Your collections sync through iCloud — in your account, not ours.

You sign in with Sign in with Apple, and your collections, items, photos, notes, dates, and locations sync through your own iCloud (CloudKit). We don't run a separate cloud copy of your content. You stay in control of your iCloud through Apple's privacy settings at all times.

Sign in with Apple iCloud (CloudKit) sync No Memry-run user database You can sign out or delete any time

1. Information we collect

Memry is designed to collect as little as possible. Depending on how you use the app, the following information may be involved:

From Sign in with Apple

When you sign in, Apple shares a unique identifier and the name and email address you choose to share (which may be a private relay address generated by Apple). We use this only to identify your account inside the app. You can revoke this access at any time in your Apple ID settings.

Stored in your iCloud (not on our servers)

Your user-created content — collection names, item names, notes, photos, dates, locations, tags, and visual preferences — is stored in your own iCloud through Apple's CloudKit framework. This means it syncs between your devices automatically, but Memry does not maintain a separate copy on its own infrastructure. Access to this data is governed by Apple's terms and your iCloud settings.

Device & app information

We may collect basic diagnostic information — app version, device type, operating system version, and anonymous crash reports — to improve app performance and stability. Where supported by iOS, this is shared via Apple's standard crash reporting tools and is not personally identifying.

Support information

If you contact us, we receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this only to respond to your request.

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Authenticate your Memry account through Sign in with Apple.
  • Provide and operate Memry's core features.
  • Allow your collections and items to sync across your devices through iCloud.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Improve app stability, design, and functionality.
  • Protect the security and integrity of the service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. User content

Your collections, photos, notes, locations, and other personal content belong to you. They are stored in your iCloud account and we do not access, sell, share, or monetize them. We do not train any model on your content.

4. Photos and location data

If you choose to add photos or location information to your items, Memry will access photos or location only with your explicit permission, granted through standard iOS system prompts. That information is saved into your iCloud as part of the item. You can revoke photo or location permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Because most of your content is stored in your own iCloud, there is little for us to share. We may share limited information only when necessary to:

  • Operate the app through Apple's platform services (Sign in with Apple, iCloud / CloudKit, push notifications).
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Comply with applicable laws or valid legal processes.
  • Protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the service.

6. Third-party services

Memry is built on Apple platform services and keeps third parties to a minimum:

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, iCloud, CloudKit, push notifications, App Store, TestFlight, crash reporting. Apple's handling of this data is governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.
  • Optional services — if we later add anything beyond Apple's platform (for example, an email provider for support replies), we will list it here and update this policy.

We do not use third-party advertising, ad networks, or cross-site trackers.

7. Data retention

Because most of your content is stored in your own iCloud, you control how long it is kept. If you delete a collection or item inside the app, it is removed from your iCloud. If you delete your iCloud account or stop syncing, the data leaves our reach.

The limited identifiers we receive through Sign in with Apple are kept only as long as needed to operate your account. We keep support correspondence as long as reasonably necessary to handle your request and comply with legal obligations.

8. Data deletion requests

You can delete your data in two ways:

  • Inside the app. Delete individual items or full collections — they are removed from your iCloud immediately. You can also revoke Sign in with Apple access at any time in your Apple ID settings.
  • By email. Contact support@memryworld.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request." We will delete any identifier we hold and confirm by email. We may need to verify your request first.

9. Children's privacy

Memry is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact support@memryworld.com.

10. Security

Memry relies on Apple's platform security: Sign in with Apple, encrypted iCloud storage and transport, and the iOS sandbox. We add reasonable safeguards on our side for the limited information we receive. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

12. Contact us

For privacy questions, support requests, or data deletion requests, contact support@memryworld.com.

For general inquiries, contact info@memryworld.com.
